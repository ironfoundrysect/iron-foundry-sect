What is the Iron Foundry Sect?

Look — we're a group of engineers and builders who got tired of learning from tutorials and never finishing anything. The Iron Foundry Sect is the fix for that. You join, you build something, you document it, and the whole community benefits.

The cultivation sect theme is half serious, half a bit of fun. But the ranking system is real — you move up by actually finishing and documenting projects, not just hanging around.

Projects are called Techniques, the workshop is The Forge, and every build gets a Manual. The library grows every time someone finishes something and actually writes it up.
