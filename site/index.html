What is the Iron Foundry Sect?

The Iron Foundry Sect is a community of engineers, builders, and makers who believe that true mastery is earned through creation — not memorization.

Like disciples of an ancient cultivation sect, our members advance through ranks of technical skill by completing real engineering projects. From circuits to code, from 3D printers to autonomous rovers.

We call our projects Techniques, our workshop The Forge, and our documentation Manuals. Because what you build should be worthy of legend.
